Home Came Lebron

LeBron James is coming back home
To Cleveland, all reCavaliered.
Forgiven for Miami roam?
The Heat has still not disappeared,
But over time should fade away,
If LeBron runs a great rebound,
And more will pay while he does play.
Winning does wonders all around.
Also rans is a bunch of 'bleep.'
So for the Cavs - not much deep thunk,
Just reached in Cav cash pockets deep.
LeBron in Cleveland - a slam dunk.

Come home prodigal son, LeBron.
Welcome back - get a title on.
